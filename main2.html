Doraemon: Nobita’s Sky Utopia Movie

The new Doraemon anime movie, Doraemon: Nobita’s Sky Utopia, has received a new, full-length trailer the same day as its Japanese premiere. The trailer shows off the titular sky utopia, Paradapia, which Nobita and his friends must help save, and the cat-like robot Sonya (Ren Nagase from King & Prince). Also previewed is the theme song “Paradise” by NiziU.
